# Bio

## Short (one line)

Senior Full Stack Engineer, founder, and AI engineer who builds products
end-to-end — from customer discovery to distributed systems.

## Medium (elevator pitch)

I'm Nikhil — a Senior Full Stack Engineer with a founder's instinct. I started in
business development, taught myself to code, built and launched my own product
(ConnectEdApp), and grew into senior engineering and AI-native development. I
understand technology, product, sales, and customers — and that combination lets
me take ideas all the way to shipped, validated products.

## Long (about narrative)

My path into engineering wasn't a straight line — and that's my advantage.

I began as a **Business Development Manager**, selling and negotiating, learning
firsthand what businesses pay for and where they hurt. I kept seeing problems
software could solve, so I taught myself to build it — React first, then the full
MERN stack.

That led me to **found ConnectEdApp**, an education platform I built and ran
single-handedly: product, engineering, sales, demos to school administrators,
customer discovery, and iteration on real feedback. I shipped it to the Google
Play Store and presented it to 100+ school administrators.

I brought that product-and-customer mindset into **senior engineering** —
architecting distributed systems, an accessibility-scanning platform, and
AI-native developer tooling. Today I'm building **AI-powered developer tools**
(MCP servers, agentic workflows, and Ignix UI), compounding everything I've
learned across business, product, and engineering.
