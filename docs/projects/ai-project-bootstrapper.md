## Overview

A scaffold that is **production-grade and AI-agent-ready out of the box**.
Opinionated AI-app templates come with `CLAUDE.md`, `AGENTS.md`, evals,
observability, guardrails, and MCP config pre-wired, plus CI — so an AI app starts
right instead of accruing the same setup debt every time.

## Problem

`create-next-app` and friends aren't AI-ready or opinionated for AI apps;
v0 / bolt.new / Lovable generate apps but not production scaffolds; Yeoman/Nx are
powerful but not AI-aware and config-heavy.

## Differentiators

1. **Agent-context files baked in** (`CLAUDE.md`, `AGENTS.md`).
2. **Evals + observability + guardrails** pre-wired.
3. **MCP config + CI** out of the box.

## Competitive landscape

create-next-app / framework CLIs (not AI-ready), v0 / bolt.new / Lovable
(consistency, not production scaffold), Yeoman / Nx generators (not AI-aware).

## Strategic note

**Best as a ContextOS feature** — weak standalone monetization, strong as the
on-ramp that wires new projects into the platform from day one.

## Risks

Commoditization of scaffolding. Mitigation: ship as a ContextOS on-ramp rather
than a standalone paid product.

## Role in the platform family

The **on-ramp**. New projects start agent-ready and pre-connected to ContextOS,
Agent Monitoring, and Codebase Intelligence.
